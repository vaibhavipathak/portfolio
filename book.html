Hindu Gods

Book Design & Illustration

Being a Hindu myself, I was surprised to learn that there was no resource available that would explain the concept of Hinduism to a young child in an easy and a correct way. All the poems, books and videos I found jumped stright to talking about some or numrous of the Hindu dieties without first introducing the concept of "One God, multiple forms".

I decided to tackle the problem myself and wrote this fun and engaging book. I created all the copy and illustrations myself while involving several religious leaders, teachers, other parents and my little helpers at every stage for testing and feedback. I have self published this book and have received nothing but good reviews so far.
